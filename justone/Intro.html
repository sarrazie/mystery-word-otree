Dear MIEPP students, let's play a game together!

You are randomly assigned to groups of four. Together in your group you play {{ if group.incentive }} to get the best score {{ else }} {{ endif }} in overall 16 turns.

Goal: One of the players – the guesser – has to guess a mystery word based on clues given by the other players.

If two or more players write the same clue, all identical clues will be canceled before the guesser gets to see them.

{{ if group.incentive }} At the end, the group with the highest score (one point for each correctly guessed mystery word) wins a prize. {{ else }} {{ endif }}

Each turn is divided in four phases:

  1. All players except the guesser are told which mystery word they have to provide a clue for.
  2. Without communicating with each other and without showing it to anyone, each cluegiver types one clue in. That clue must be composed of a single word.
  3. Once all player have written their clues (within max two minutes) and the identical or invalid clues have been cancelled, the guesser is shown all remaining clues and has only one guess to identify the mystery word.
  4. The group is told whether the guesser guessed the mystery word correctly or not. The next turn begins with an other player becoming the new guesser.
